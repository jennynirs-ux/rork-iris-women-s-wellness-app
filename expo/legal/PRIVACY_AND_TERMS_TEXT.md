# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: April 2026**

## 1. What Data We Collect

IRIS collects only the information you choose to share with us. This includes:

- Cycle dates and period tracking data
- Daily check-in data: mood, energy, sleep, and symptoms
- Profile information: name, age, life stage, and language preference
- Cycle length and last period date

When you use the wellness scan, IRIS uses your camera to briefly capture an
image of your eye area. The image is processed entirely on your device to
estimate wellness indicators (energy, hydration, recovery, irritation, focus
readiness, eye moisture, and similar wellness signals). The photo is
immediately discarded after analysis — it is never stored, saved, or sent to
any server. Only the resulting numerical wellness scores are kept.

## 2. How Your Data Is Stored

Your health data — including cycle dates, check-ins, mood, symptoms, and scan
scores — is stored locally on your device. Your most sensitive wellness
information never leaves your phone unless you explicitly choose to sync it.

We do not store your health data on external servers. **Your body, your data,
your device.**

## 3. Analytics & App Improvement

To improve IRIS, we collect anonymous usage events (such as which features
are opened and how often). These analytics are sent securely to our own
backend hosted in the EU (Stockholm) on Supabase Postgres. They **do not
include any personal health data** and are linked only to a randomly generated
user ID that has no connection to your name, email, or device identifiers.

Examples of what we track: app opens, feature usage counts, onboarding
completion. Examples of what we never track: your mood scores, symptoms,
cycle dates, or scan photos.

## 4. Payments & Subscriptions

If you subscribe to IRIS Premium, your payment is handled entirely by
RevenueCat and the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We never see or
store your credit card details, billing address, or payment method. For
details on how payment data is handled, please refer to RevenueCat's privacy
policy and your app store's terms.

## 5. We Never Sell Your Data

IRIS does not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for
advertising or marketing purposes. Period. Your wellness information is yours
alone.

We do not use your data to build advertising profiles, and we do not allow
third-party trackers in the app.

## 6. Your Rights & Contact

You have the right to:

- View all your stored data within the app
- Export your data at any time from Settings
- Delete all your data permanently via Settings → Delete Account

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how your data is
handled, please reach out to us at:

**jenny.nirs@mojjo.se**

We're a small team and we genuinely care about your trust.

*IRIS is developed by Mojjo SE, Sweden. Governed by Swedish law and the GDPR.*

# Terms of Service

**Last updated: April 2026**

## 1. What IRIS Is (and Isn't)

IRIS is a wellness awareness tool designed to help you understand your body's
natural rhythms and patterns over time. It is **not a medical device**, and
nothing in the app should be interpreted as a medical diagnosis, treatment
plan, or clinical measurement.

The wellness estimates, cycle phase information, and daily snapshots are based
on the data you provide through check-ins and brief scans — they are general
wellness indicators, not medical results. Always consult a qualified
healthcare professional for medical decisions.

## 2. The Wellness Scan

The eye scan feature in IRIS captures a brief image of your eye area and
analyzes it on your device to estimate wellness indicators such as energy,
hydration, recovery, fatigue, and irritation. The analysis examines general
image properties (brightness, color balance, reflection quality, skin tone)
— it is not a clinical measurement of your eyes, pupils, or any biomarkers.

Your wellness scores blend the eye analysis with your self-reported check-in
data and cycle phase for context. The photo is immediately discarded after
analysis and is never stored or transmitted.

## 3. Who Can Use IRIS

You must be at least 13 years old to use IRIS. By using the app, you confirm
that you meet this age requirement. If you are under 18, we encourage you to
use IRIS with a parent or guardian's awareness.

## 4. Subscriptions & Payments

IRIS offers optional premium features through subscriptions managed by the
Apple App Store or Google Play Store via RevenueCat. Subscriptions auto-renew
unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period.

You can manage or cancel your subscription anytime through your device's app
store settings. We do not process payments directly — all billing is handled
by Apple or Google.

## 5. Your Responsibilities

You agree to use IRIS for personal wellness tracking only and not for any
unlawful purpose. You are responsible for the accuracy of the data you enter
and for keeping your device secure.

Please do not rely solely on IRIS for health-related decisions. If you
